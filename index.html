About ERES

Re-Enrollment System was established to streamline the subject repetition process for students. The system is designed to assist students in identifying eligible subjects based on their current semester parity (odd/even) and allows them to apply for re-enrollment in courses where they secured a grade of ‘D’ or ‘F’.

Our Vision:

"To ensure a fair, efficient, and transparent re-enrollment process, empowering students to improve their academic performance while upholding integrity and accessibility."
